What Is SpotiTools

SpotiTools is a lightweight desktop application designed to enhance your Spotify experience. With SpotiTools, you can easily view the current song and use hotkeys to control playback, making it perfect for multitasking and improving productivity.






Simple and sleek GUI

The GUI is simple, sleek, and easy to use, expanding and shrinking to fit your needs.
